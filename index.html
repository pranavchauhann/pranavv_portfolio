Built the HR domain of an enterprise business-management platform modeled on Keka HRMS: 7+ Flutter submodules (Dashboard, Employee Management, Leave, Attendance, Performance) with card-based UI, plus Java/Spring Boot backend services — attendance regularization, leave balance/ledger APIs, expense approval workflows, and payroll APIs, with JPA repositories, Liquibase migrations, and unit tests. Also shipped the iOS build via Xcode.

Indian Equity Price Forecasting — LSTM vs. Baselines

End-to-end ML pipeline forecasting next-day prices for 40 NSE stocks across 4 sectors, comparing LSTM against Naive, Linear Regression, and ARIMA with leakage-free chronological splits. Finding: LSTM matched simpler models on directional accuracy (~50% — a coin flip) while producing 3-4x higher price error, traced to output-scaler extrapolation failure on prices beyond the training range. Deployed as an interactive Streamlit dashboard.

Personal Ride-Data Analysis Dashboard

Analyzed my personal Uber ride export (47 trips, Sep 2024–Aug 2025): 70% of trips ran the same home→office route; a 36% cancellation rate traced to wait times (~10.8 min average ETA on canceled requests vs ~5.1 min on completed); surge-tagged trips cost ~8% more per mile once trip mix was controlled for.
